The Clean Planet Plan

Clean Planet is an organization that is dedicated to teach the world about the effects that trash and global warming have
on our earth. Our app, also called Clean Planet, is a video game where you have to pick up trash, and it was made to spread
awareness about this problem all around the world, and we get a decent amount of income from ads and premium in game purchases.
Schools also pay us to add our game to their electronics, to teach their students about the negative problems that too much
trash brings.
