Our Games Mission:

Our buisness is dedicated to spreading awareness about global warming and the abundant amount of trash in the world. Trash
polution is a big problem, and many people either don’t know what to do about it, or don’t try to make an impact. Our solution
to this world wide problem is a video game called Clean Planet. It teaches people about the negative effects of trash pollution
through a fun trash-pickup game. It also spreads awareness and motivates people to help their community protect the environment.
Ages range 7-20, but ages 40 do like to play it sometimes to kill time.


Not everyone is aware of the global warming problem, and thankfully, our company noticed this.
Global warming is an effect from humans. We burn fossil fuels, throw trash, and don't recycle, which heats up the earth.
The earth is getting warmer each year, and we know it. Our buisness is doing everything it can to make the world a better place.
